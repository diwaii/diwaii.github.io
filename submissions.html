Preliminary Requirements for Submission

Anyone doing psychology-related research is welcome to submit! First, please read the submission requirements here for important details about pre-publication screening, conflicts of interest, etc, before following the link at the bottom of the page to submit your article.

  • Must generally be about Psychology and Psychology-related topics.
  • At least 10 references for empirical papers, and at least 12 references for literature reviews (more than 15 is preferred).
  • Must mostly follow the structure and format of a Psychology research paper, which includes having an Abstract, an Introduction, a Methods Section, a Results Section, a Discussion/Limitations section, and References. This also means a paper eligible for review should be mostly-adherent to APA formatting, even if there are some mistakes.
  • Must make citations for most claims (preferably all claims) and generally communicate with language that is appropriate for Psychology research.
  • Must be longer than 5 pages (not including references) and must be shorter than 25 pages (not including references). If your paper was longer than 25 pages but you have otherwise met criteria 1-3 above, please consider submitting your work to other journals! You may qualify for submission there.

Submission Process

Once you have determined you meet the preliminary requirements detailed above, you are ready for submission! Simply fill out our Google Form shown below, and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
